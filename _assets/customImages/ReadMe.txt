Here you can create folders by theme name (megadrive, snes, neogeo etc..) and put images files at low resolution (for example 240p to 360p).

Images must be named:

	-"01.jpg", "02.jpg", "03.jpg", "04.jpg", "05.jpg", "06.jpg", "07.jpg".

		or

	-"01.png", "02.png", "03.png", "04.png", "05.png", "06.png", "07.png".

They will displayed in the 7 tiles of systemview layer in order from upper left (01.jpg) to bottom right (07.jpg).
